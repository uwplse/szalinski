Szalinski Demo

Szalinski is a tool for automatically inferring loops in the form of Folds, Maps, and Tabulates from flat CSG based 3D models. Szalinski uses Equality Saturation, a powerful compiler optimization technique together with a new idea called Inverse Transformations to automatically find loops.
